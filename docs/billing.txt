TechNova Billing and Pricing

TechNova offers three plans: Basic ($9/month), Pro ($29/month) and Enterprise (custom pricing).

To upgrade or downgrade your plan, go to Settings > Billing > Change Plan.

Refunds are available within 14 days of purchase. To request a refund contact billing@technova.com with your order number.

To cancel your subscription go to Settings > Billing > Cancel Subscription. Your account will remain active until the end of the billing period.

Payment methods accepted are Visa, Mastercard and PayPal.
