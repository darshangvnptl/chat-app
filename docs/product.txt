TechNova Product Overview

TechNova is a SaaS platform that helps teams manage projects, collaborate in real time and track productivity.

Key features include task management, team chat, file sharing, time tracking and reporting dashboards.

TechNova integrates with Slack, Google Workspace, Microsoft Teams and GitHub.

The platform is available on web, iOS and Android.

For technical issues contact support@technova.com or visit our help centre at help.technova.com.

System status and outages are reported at status.technova.com.
